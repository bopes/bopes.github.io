About Me

I was born in Charlotte, North Carolina. during a hurricane, and lived there until I graduated high school. I then attended Duke University for college where I received a BS in Mathematics and a minor in Economics. After graduating, I moved back to Charlotte and worked on the sales team at Bridgetree, a marketing support firm specializing in direct marketing, data analytics and web application development.

For fun, I like to go to the nearby Appalachain mountains, and my dream is to live in a remote cabin on a lake, where I can build out new additions to my cabin and play on the water when the sun comes out.

I listen to a lot of country music, but only the good stuff. Johnny Cash and Emmylou Harris trump the CMA's every day of the week. I also watch a lot of movies and TV, and believe Mad Max: Fury Road is one of the greatest cinematic achievements of my lifetime. Akira Kurosawa was pretty good, too.
